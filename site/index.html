Brief biography

Leadership, tech, and storytelling in one timetable

I'm a Computer Science student at Miva Open University, currently interning with SaNDAI DataGen Scholars on generative AI research and with Action Academy, learning how democracy and civil rights translate into real change. Outside the classroom, I've spent the last few years leading — as Former President of the Fellowship of Christian Students, and as Provincial Teens Prayer Coordinator and Regional President for RCCG Region 46, where I've helped run programmes for 400+ teens and now manage the region's finances as Head of Finance for PSF Region 46.

In 2022 I founded Life Box, a research project on autism awareness, and I organise Teens Hangout With Fofa, an annual meet-up for teens and youths to connect and worship together. When I'm not in a meeting or a lab, I'm writing — poems, scripts for animated fanfiction, and the odd scholarship guide for younger students I mentor.
